Hi!

I'm a game programmer who is passionate about learning everything I can about game programming. I like trying to understand everything that goes on under the hood even when using ready made engines like Unity or Unreal. I still have a long way to go, but I am enjoying the process every step of the way. I believe in writing code with good performance whenever possible. I try to follow data-oriented programming principles in my own and any work projects I do.
