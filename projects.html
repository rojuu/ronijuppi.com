The Land of the Scurvy Dog

The Land of the Scruvy Dog is a 3D platformer where you play as a pirate corgi. The game focuses on smooth controls and challenging platforming. The game is strictly a demo, or a prototype at this point; free for anyone to play and evaluate. The game was made using Unreal Engine 4 and is available on PC. We made this game for our fall project course in 2016.

What I contributed to this project:
  • Gameplay logic programming
  • Asset and animation implementation
  • Loads of research as this was our first UE4 project

Insult to Injury

Insult to Injury is a game about coming up with the best insults. The goal of the game is to insult your opponent in the worst ways. The game features online and local multiplayer. You can also take a shot at beating the AI opponent. The game also has a lot of hats. The game was made in Unity for Android during our spring project course in 2016.

What stuff I did in this project:
  • Implementing online features (match-making, cloud save, etc.)
  • Content loading and management (word packs, hats)
  • Gameplay implementation
  • Bunch of little polish

Gravity Creatures

Gravity Creatures is a mobile puzzle game with 40 levels and a bunch of customization and unlockables. The game is divided in to 10 biomes. Each biome has a new gimmick introduced in it. The game was made in Unity for Android during our summer proejct course in 2016.

What I did for this project:
  • Most of the UI code
  • Content loading and management
  • Gameplay code and polish stuff
